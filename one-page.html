P.E.O.P.L.E. are artists.

We are a multilayered organization dedicated to redefining the boundaries of art and media through the use of AI and emerging technologies. P.E.O.P.L.E. is an ongoing work of art expressed through the vessel of a company, using language, code, and culture as material. We challenge the dominance of intermediaries in film, music, and media production industries, emphasizing their dependency on artists as their greatest vulnerability and our greatest strength.

Our work aims to disrupt notions of intellectual property, authenticity, and identity by using data collection, model training, image and text generation; art as an act of aesthetic terrorism. We treat these methods as vectors for asymmetrical attacks on outmoded systems of cultural creation, generation, and media production hegemony. Our goal is to disrupt and transform the system.

We have over 15 years of experience in art, media production, design, AI, programming, and web3. Our projects include creating AI-based algorithms for graphics generation, integrating AI technologies into graphic design tools, and optimizing algorithms for performance and scalability.

Our art, tools and dataset renegotiate the mimetic landscape and a media/production industry that has atrophied into a generic process of cultural and technical templating. Our work reconfigures industries like adult entertainment, film, the art market, and fashion, aiming to turn art into a fluid mechanism to reshape the media environment and systems of distribution.

AI represents the next evolution in human storytelling. P.E.O.P.L.E. is shaping the human story.
